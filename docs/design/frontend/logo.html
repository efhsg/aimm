The Concept: "The Validation Gate"

The AIMM symbol is built on the principle of Data Quality over Speed. It visualizes the rigorous three-phase pipeline: Collect, Analyze, Render.

The outer structure creates an architectural 'A'—stable, institutional, and precise. It represents the raw volume of market data collected.

The inner aperture forms a Teal Chevron or "Gate". This represents the validation layer. Data does not pass to the top of the pyramid until it clears this checkpoint. It is the visual promise of provenance and accuracy.
